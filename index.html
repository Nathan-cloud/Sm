We believe that...

There is an imbalance of accessing digital educational content by Sub Saharan Africa rural communities and schools due to low level of internet penetration and its high cost. Rural youth barely can afford the internet for educational and learning purposes. Over 650m people live in rural Africa with over 70% youth that barely access the internet. Creating opportunities for rural youth in Africa, thereâ€™s a high need of democratizing access to educational and learning digital content.

Introducing SomaBox.

SomaBox is an open source, portable digital file distribution tool based on inexpensive hardware that enables delivery of educational content and other vital information to individuals/schools off the grid. SomaBox has the ability where students can access handpicked educational content without needing access to the internet. Same applies with teachers where they are able to create educational content which can be uploaded on Somabox and students access it within the given LAN.

How it works

Users need to connect to their disposable devices as it is done connecting to the wifi and start to access available uploaded content without needing the internet at all.

Our Target

We intend to scale this product to all Sub Saharan Countries by partnering with education boards to provide their content of their particula curricula. We will avail our technology as an open source for schools to develop their custom made devices.
